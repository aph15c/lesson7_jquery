New York Style Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 18 graham crackers, crushed
  • 1/4 c flour
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 c sour cream
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 8oz. packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 c white sugar
  • 1 2/3 c milk
  • 1 tsp finely grated lemon zest
  • 1 tsp finely grated lemon zest

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350.

Lightly grease the bottom and sides of a 9-inch springform pan.

Mix graham cracker crumbs and butter in a bowl. Press crumb mixture into the bottom and about 1/2-inch up the sides of the pan.

Whisk flour, sour cream, and vanilla extract in bowl. Set aside.

Stir cream cheese and sugar with a wooden spoon in a large bowl until even.

Pour milk into cream cheese mixture and whisk until combined.

Whisk in eggs, one at a time, stirring well after each addition.

Stir in lemon zest, orange zest, and sour cream mixture; whisk until just incorporated.

Pour mixture into pan.

Bake until the edges have evenly puffed and the surface of the cheesecake is firm with jiggly center. Around 1 hour.

When done, turn off oven and let cake cool in the oven for 3 to 4 hours to prevent cracks on cake.
